Delicious Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (for boiling pasta)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente according to package instructions.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat a pan over medium heat and cook diced pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino Romano, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  6. Immediately toss the hot pasta with the egg and cheese mixture, allowing the heat to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Add the crispy pancetta or guanciale and mix well.
  8. If needed, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency.
  9. Season with freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  10. Serve immediately and enjoy!
